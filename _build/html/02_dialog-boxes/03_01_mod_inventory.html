Species inventory#

Assumptions, Pros, Cons
Assumptions

  • No formal assumptions (Wearn & Glover-Kapfer, 2017)

Pros

  • Maximum flexibility for study design (e.g., camera days per camera location or use of lure (Rovero et al., 2013)) (Wearn & Glover-Kapfer, 2017)

Cons

  • Not reliable estimates for inference (‘considered as unfinished, working drafts’) (Wearn & Glover-Kapfer, 2017)

Inventory: Rapid assessment surveys to determine what species are present in a given area at a given point in time; there is no attempt made to quantify aspects of communities or populations (Wearn & Glover-Kapfer, 2017).
